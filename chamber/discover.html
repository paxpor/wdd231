Discover Pocatello

History

Pocatello grew as a transportation and trade hub in southeastern Idaho. Over time it developed into a center for education, business, and community life.

Demographics

Pocatello is home to families, students, small business owners, and a growing regional workforce. It is known for its welcoming community and access to outdoor recreation.

Current Events

  • Farmer's Market opens in spring
  • Local business networking nights downtown
  • Seasonal art and music events
