Website Planning Document

Site Name

Grandpa & Son Workshop

This name reflects a family-based woodworking business focused on handmade craftsmanship. It emphasizes tradition, quality, and the relationship between generations, which is central to the brand identity.

Optional domain: grandpaandsonworkshop.com

Site Purpose

The website serves as an online storefront and informational hub for handmade wooden products. Visitors can browse products such as pens, bowls, and jewelry boxes, learn about the story behind the workshop, and contact the creators for custom orders or inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What types of handmade products are available for purchase?
  • How can I request a custom woodworking item?
  • What is the story behind the workshop and who makes the products?
  • How much do the products cost and how do I order them?

Color Scheme

  • #3a5a40 (Primary Green) – Used for headings, navigation, and branding
  • #8b5e3c (Wood Brown Accent) – Used for buttons and highlights
  • #fbfbf9 (Background) – Main page background
  • #ffffff (Card Background) – Sections and content containers

Typography

  • Poppins – Used for headings and body text
  • Fallback fonts: Arial, sans-serif

Poppins was selected for its clean, modern appearance while still feeling friendly and approachable, matching the handmade and personal nature of the brand.
